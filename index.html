2 player Pong:

Instructions:

Pong is a game with 2 paddles and a ball. The goal of
the game is to try and keep the ball from going past
your side. If the ball goes past the opponents side,
you gain a point. The first person to get 10 points
wins the game. Good Luck!

Controls:

Paddle 1 - WASD
Paddle 2 - Arrow Keys
