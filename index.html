2 player Pong:

Instructions:

Pong is a game with 2 "bats" and a ball. The goal of
the game is to try and keep the ball from going past
your bat. If the ball goes past the opponents bat,
you gain a point. The first person to get 10 points
wins the game. Good Luck!

Controls:

Bat 1 - Arrow Keys
Bat 2 - WASD
